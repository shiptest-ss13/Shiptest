20 September 2021

ZephyrTFA updated:

  • Weapon Rechargers no longer enter a permanent state of invisibility upon being smacked with a screwdriver.

untrius updated:

  • Survey handhelds let you know if/why they failed and make noise

19 September 2021

MarkSuckerberg updated:

  • You can now pick up Teshari into mob holders. Teshari and other holdable mobs cannot pick up other holdable mobs to prevent recursion of any kind.
  • Two new coloured customization options for Tesh, including head feathers/ears and optional body colour.
  • Teshari now have a unique language (with no unique sprite as of yet) called Schechi.
  • You can now only put one mob holder into a duffel bag, and no other bags.
  • Teshari actually have eyes now
  • You can no longer eat some simplemobs alive (if you know, you know)
  • mob holders can now breathe in bags
  • Minor Teshari diet adjustment, they now prefer meat and dislike grain/gross foods.
  • Teshari blood is no longer ammonia.

17 September 2021

Ms-Mee updated:

  • Removed prescriptionHUDs from gear and made them be given by the nearsighted quirk instead, which doesn't override your starting glasses anymore.
  • No more roundstart permanent HUD from deleted gear item.

PrefabQuasar updated:

  • Added the ability to dip stock parts into certain reagents, converting them into other reagents
  • Added the reagent analyzer, craftable from a health analyzer, and the seperatory funnel, craftable from 3 small beakers
  • Added two new "medicine" reagents, which convert between brute and burn, however, unlike convermol, total damage will INCREASE, making them only situationally useful
  • made the electrolysis reaction use less liquid electricity to convert the same amount of water
  • Chemistry can now be done without chemistry machines
  • Added icons for the reagent analyzer, and the seperatory funnel

mcmeiler updated:

  • Added midround Jellyperson customization! Included are: Mutant Color, hair style, facial hair style, ears and tails. (More may follow.)
  • Sprites for slime cat ears and tails
