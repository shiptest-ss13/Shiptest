MemedHams updated:

  • Adds a wolf talisman that can be crafted from both wolf trophies.

  • Adds unique trophies for the ancient goliath, dwarf legion, and ash drake(old ash drake trophy is now the ice whelp trophy)
  • goliath tentacle crusher trophy now works as intended, instead of doing nothing.

MemedHams updated:

  • A particularly drippy outfit. Don't take it off!

  • Ports the Ice Cube artifact from /TG/

  • Redesigns Hook Shot item to apply fast attacks in melee range.
  • Added new pathing to some necro items, and reorganized list to contain two less items (for now)
  • Removed Jacob's Ladder and Dark Blessing from the necropolis loot pool.
  • Added buffed rework of possessed blade to necroloot pool, gaining amplified damage, block chance, and bloodletting
  • Reworks cursed blade to cause acts of hubris and occasionally painful death
  • Buffed all the elite items from useless to ok, since you can only get them in the first place by fighting a pvp megafauna
  • gave the necropolis chest power miner a small chance to drop a chooseable version, instead. Spin the wheel of overwhelmingly low chances.
  • removes ability to choose gravokinetic guardian until the game-breaking visual bugs are fixed.
